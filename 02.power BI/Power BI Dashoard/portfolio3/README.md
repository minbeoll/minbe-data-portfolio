   This dashboard aims to help management and operational staff quickly comprehend the current business situation and provide key insights needed to make decisions for the future by connecting revenue growth, customer structure, and target achievement into a single flow.

  It is built with a structure that follows revenue increase and decrease and growth, the quality of customer structure, and profit compared with targets. It is capable of supporting not only metric analysis but also business decision-making in real business circumstances.
