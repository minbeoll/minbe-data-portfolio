    This Power BI dashboard is a management analysis report focused on real-world practice, built to analyze revenue achievement, customer and order structure, and target achievement from an end-to-end perspective.

On page 1, you can understand how current business performance—centered on total revenue growth—is changing compared to the previous year.

On page 2, the dashboard explains whether revenue growth is driven by an influx of new customers or by existing customers, by analyzing the structure of new and repeat customers.

On page 3, by managing target achievement rates and achievement variance through comparisons between actual revenue and target revenue, the dashboard allows users to assess the probability of achieving future targets using rolling and running rate measures.

This dashboard is not a simple visualization but a Power BI report built to help management and operational staff quickly comprehend the current situation and make informed decisions.
